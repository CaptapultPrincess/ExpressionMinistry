About Me

Shelantea

Shelantea is a creative expressionist from Evansville In. She is a wife, mother, and serves as an Administrative Coordinator. When she is not singing, inspiring, or writing songs and creative works, she is mentoring or catching up on world news. Shelantea is passionate about inspiring the next generation about their future legacy, heritage past, and current unstoppable potential. You can find these themes woven into her musical art and creative expressions that make her ministry so rich.
